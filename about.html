Every Fractional CMO's Nightmare

You hire a fractional CMO for $15,000/month. They promise a comprehensive audit and strategic roadmap. Week one, they're excited, gathering data, interviewing stakeholders. Then reality hits.

"I was hired to transform their marketing strategy. Six months later, I was still fighting daily fires and hadn't completed the audit I promised in week one." — Every fractional CMO, ever

The Firefighting Timeline

Week 1
Campaign Emergency

Major campaign launching next week. Drop everything to fix messaging, targeting, and creative.

Week 2
Website Crisis

Conversion rates tanking. Urgent A/B tests needed. Audit postponed.

Week 3
Sales Team Revolt

Lead quality complaints. Emergency meeting. New scoring system needed yesterday.

Week 4
Board Presentation

CEO needs marketing metrics for board meeting. Three days to build dashboards from scratch.

Month 6
Still No Audit

That comprehensive audit and strategy? Still sitting at 20% complete.

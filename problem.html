Why Even Great CMOs Fail as Fractionals

It's not incompetence — it's the model itself

⏱️

Part-Time Presence

Problems don't wait for your CMO's scheduled days. By the time they're back, small fires have become infernos.

🎭

Context Switching Hell

Managing 3-4 clients means never going deep enough. Surface-level understanding leads to surface-level solutions.

📊

Manual Analysis Bottleneck

A proper audit takes 80-120 hours. At 2 days/week, that's 2-3 months — if nothing interrupts.

🚨

No Early Warning System

Without continuous monitoring, every problem becomes an emergency by the time it's discovered.

🔄

The Urgency Trap

Urgent always beats important. Strategic work requires deep focus — impossible when everything's on fire.

😤

Stakeholder Pressure

"We're paying $15K/month!" creates pressure for immediate visible action, not invisible strategy work.

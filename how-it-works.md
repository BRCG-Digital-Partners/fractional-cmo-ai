# How Meridian AI Works

## The Big Picture

Meridian AI solves the fractional CMO paradox: CMOs are hired for strategy but spend 80%+ of their time firefighting. Our platform completes comprehensive marketing audits in 2 hours (vs 3-6 months manually) and deploys AI agents to handle routine tasks, freeing CMOs to focus on strategy.

## Technical Architecture

### 1. Discovery Engine
When you enter a URL, our AI agents immediately begin discovering your entire digital footprint:

- **Website Crawler**: Analyzes all pages, meta tags, content, performance metrics
- **Social Discovery**: Finds all social media profiles and analyzes engagement
- **Ad Platform Detection**: Identifies Google Ads, Meta, LinkedIn campaigns via pixels/tags
- **Competitor Intelligence**: Discovers top 5 competitors and their strategies
- **Tech Stack Analysis**: Identifies all marketing tools and integrations

### 2. Analysis Engine
Our AI analyzes 150+ checkpoints across 8 marketing domains:

- **SEO & Content**: Technical SEO, content quality, keyword opportunities, rankings
- **Performance**: Page speed, Core Web Vitals, mobile optimization, server response
- **Paid Advertising**: Campaign structure, targeting, budget efficiency, ROAS
- **Email Marketing**: List health, automation flows, deliverability, engagement
- **Social Media**: Platform presence, posting frequency, engagement rates
- **Analytics & Data**: Tracking setup, conversion goals, attribution, reporting
- **Brand & Messaging**: Consistency, positioning, voice, competitive differentiation
- **Sales Alignment**: Lead quality, funnel metrics, enablement materials

### 3. AI Agent System
Specialized AI agents that can be deployed with one click:

**SEO Agent**
- Generates and submits XML sitemaps
- Creates robots.txt with optimal rules
- Implements JSON-LD schema markup
- Fixes meta titles/descriptions
- Optimizes header structure
- Monitors rankings and technical issues

**Content Agent**
- Analyzes content gaps vs competitors
- Generates SEO-optimized blog posts
- Updates stale content automatically
- Creates content calendars
- Implements internal linking
- Optimizes for featured snippets

**Performance Agent**
- Compresses images automatically
- Minifies CSS/JavaScript files
- Implements browser caching rules
- Enables lazy loading for images
- Optimizes server response times
- Sets up CDN configurations

**PPC Agent**
- Creates optimized campaign structures
- Implements conversion tracking
- A/B tests ad copy and creative
- Manages bid strategies
- Monitors quality scores
- Generates performance reports

**Email Agent**
- Sets up welcome series flows
- Creates abandoned cart sequences
- Implements list segmentation
- Monitors deliverability metrics
- A/B tests subject lines
- Manages preference centers

**Analytics Agent**
- Implements proper GA4 setup
- Creates conversion goals
- Sets up enhanced ecommerce
- Builds custom dashboards
- Implements server-side tracking
- Creates attribution models

### 4. Monitoring System
24/7 intelligent monitoring that only alerts for strategic issues:

- **Smart Filtering**: AI determines what requires human attention vs auto-fix
- **Pattern Recognition**: Identifies trends and anomalies across all channels
- **Competitive Intelligence**: Monitors competitor moves and market changes
- **Predictive Alerts**: Warns of potential issues before they become problems
- **Strategic Insights**: Surfaces growth opportunities and threats
